Why Us ?

UPVC KEY FEATURES

1

HEAT INSULATION

Premier’s windows keeps efficiently cold air from outside by sealing structure and multi-chamber design.

2

SOUND INSULATION

Premier’s window provide excellent sound insulation through use of special hardware and gasket between frames to provide more pleasant and comfortable interior space.

3

AIR INFILTRATION

When the amount of air that passes through unit area per unit hour is measured, the amount for Premier’s window is less compared with traditional window because of unique profile welding system.

4

WIND LOAD

Most of the claims and defects in breakage window by a strong wind due to using low-quality window. Premier’s window is much stronger by multi-chamber structure frame and strengthened profile by steel reinforcement.

5

WATER TIGHTNESS

Even the small amount of water can cause huge damage to home interior. Premier’s window doesn’t leak water throughout joint parts due to unique fusion welding system and specially designed stair type structure.
